## 1. iPhone Screenshots

| Display | Devices | Portrait | Landscape |
|---|---|---|---|
| 6.9" | iPhone Air, 17 Pro Max, 16 Pro Max, 16 Plus, 15 Pro Max, 15 Plus, 14 Pro Max | 1260x2736 | 2736x1260 |
| 6.5" | iPhone 14 Plus, 13 Pro Max, 12 Pro Max, 11 Pro Max, 11, XS Max, XR | 1284x2778 or 1242x2688 | 2778x1284 or 2688x1242 |
| 6.3" | iPhone 17 Pro, 17, 16 Pro, 16, 15 Pro, 15, 14 Pro | 1179x2556 or 1206x2622 | 2556x1179 or 2622x1206 |
| 6.1" | iPhone 17e, 16e, 14, 13 Pro, 13, 12 Pro, 12, 11 Pro, XS, X | 1170x2532 or 1125x2436 or 1080x2340 | Matching landscape |
| 5.5" | iPhone 8 Plus, 7 Plus, 6S Plus | 1242x2208 | 2208x1242 |

**Required:** At least 6.9" OR 6.5" screenshots. Smaller sizes scale from larger.

## 2. iPad Screenshots

| Display | Devices | Portrait | Landscape |
|---|---|---|---|
| 13" | iPad Pro (M5/M4), iPad Air (M4/M3/M2) | 2064x2752 or 2048x2732 | 2752x2064 or 2732x2048 |
| 11" | iPad Pro, iPad Air, iPad (A16), iPad mini | 1488x2266 or 1668x2388 | Matching landscape |

**Required if app runs on iPad:** At least 13" screenshots.

## 3. Other Platforms

| Platform | Size |
|---|---|
| Mac | 2880x1800 or 2560x1600 or 1440x900 or 1280x800 (16:10 ratio) |
| Apple TV | 3840x2160 or 1920x1080 |
| Apple Vision Pro | 3840x2160 |
| Apple Watch | Varies by model (396x484 for Series 9/8/7, 416x496 for Series 11/10) |

## 4. General Rules

- Format: PNG or JPEG
- Quantity: 1-10 per device type per locale
- If a specific size is missing, App Store scales from the next compatible size
- Screenshots must show actual app content (no marketing overlays required but allowed)
- Must match current app version
